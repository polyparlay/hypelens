# Influencer research — how to find the right targets

You're looking for **500-10K follower accounts who actively post about Polymarket bets**. Mega-accounts ignore DMs. Bots and pure crypto-shillers won't convert.

## Search queries that work on X

Run these in X search (Latest tab), then filter manually:

| Query | Why |
|---|---|
| `polymarket bet OR parlay` | Direct intent — people actively trading PM |
| `polymarket from:0` (replace 0 with your handle) | Find accounts in YOUR follower network already PM-curious |
| `min_faves:50 polymarket` | Posts with engagement → poster has audience traction |
| `prediction market site:polymarket.com` | Often catches power users referencing PM in their bios |
| `polymarket alpha OR edge` | Sophisticated PM traders (highest-value targets) |
| `kelly criterion polymarket` | Math-aware bettors (perfect for our positioning) |
| `polymarket profit OR pnl OR P&L` | Reveals actual traders, not just commenters |

## Vetting checklist (30 seconds per profile)

Open each potential target and check:

- [ ] **>500 followers**, ideally 1K-10K (sweet spot for response rate)
- [ ] **Posted about PM in the last 7 days** (not just historical mention)
- [ ] **Real human** (varied content, replies to others, not just RTs)
- [ ] **DMs are open** (look for "Message" button on profile; if absent, you'll need to reply publicly)
- [ ] **Not already a paid PolyParlay user** (search your wallet logs)

If 4+ ticks → pass to DM list.
If 3 ticks → maybe, depending on shortage of targets.
If ≤2 ticks → skip; not worth a comp code.

## Specific account TYPES to prioritize (in order)

1. **Solo PM bettors with public PnL** — they screenshot their wallets, often have a Substack
2. **Sports-bettor-turned-PM** — they understand parlays + EV math intuitively
3. **Crypto-native quant types** — pay in USDC without thinking; high LTV
4. **Forecasting hobbyists** (Manifold, Metaculus crowds) — they appreciate Monte Carlo
5. **Political-junkie PM users** — usually active when there's a hot political market

## DM volume target

- **Week 1**: 10 DMs total (test the script, refine)
- **Week 2**: 15 DMs (scale what's working)
- **Week 3+**: 5/day sustainable cadence

**Total ~50 DMs over 30 days.** At 10-20% activation rate, that's 5-10 active influencers — enough to drive a measurable second-order traffic wave.

## Don't bother with

- **Generic crypto influencers** (Crypto Twitter "alpha" accounts) — they spam comp offers from every project, your code goes unused.
- **PM ENGAGEMENT FARMERS** (accounts that only retweet PM news without commentary) — no original audience.
- **Anyone with "DM me for promo" in bio** — they're paid-promo accounts. If you want paid promos, that's a different budget question.
- **Accounts that post >10×/day** — they're bots or full-time poster-managers; your DM gets buried.

## When in doubt

Send the DM. Comp codes cost you $0 marginal. Worst case: they ignore it. Best case: a 5K-follower account tweets about your product with the slip URL embedded and you get 200 installs overnight. Asymmetric upside — be liberal with sends.
