HypeLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-12

HypeLens is a read-only analysis overlay for the Hyperliquid trading app. It is built to collect as little as possible — in the read-only build, nothing personally identifying is collected, stored remotely, or transmitted to us.

What we collect

Nothing. We have no servers that receive your data, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. We do not know who you are.

What is stored, and where

Your local preferences only — selected coin, heatmap intensity/opacity, and (if you enter one) a wallet address to watch in read-only Guardian mode. These live on your own device via chrome.storage.local and never leave it.

Network requests

HypeLens makes requests only to Hyperliquid's public API (api.hyperliquid.xyz) to fetch public market data (prices, candles, funding, open interest) and public on-chain wallet positions. These are the same public endpoints the Hyperliquid app itself uses. No request carries any private key or authentication, and no request is sent to any HypeLens-operated server.

Your wallet

The read-only build does not connect to your wallet, request signatures, hold keys, or place orders. Guardian mode reads a wallet address only as public text (from the page or one you paste) to look up that address's public positions. Reading a public address is not a wallet connection.

Permissions

storage for local preferences; access to api.hyperliquid.xyz for public data; injection on app.hyperliquid.xyz to render the overlay. Nothing broader.

Third parties

None. We do not sell, share, or transmit data to anyone. There is no data to sell.

Not financial advice

HypeLens is informational. Leveraged trading carries substantial risk; you can lose all funds. Liquidation clusters are estimates of where leverage concentrates and no tool can predict price or prevent liquidation.

Contact

Questions: open an issue on the HypeLens repository or contact the address listed on the store page.
